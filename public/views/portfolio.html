Office Storage

I was excited to help beepi, a Bay Area start-up to get their storage in gear. In a productive work environment, it's important to be able to find what you need quickly. No more wasting time hunting throughout the office for what you're looking for. It's easy now that things are placed in clear bins and conveniently located in accessible areas with added labels for easy retrieval. Being able to view an accurate amount of surplus helps to eliminate over purchasing of necessary equipment and supplies.

Garage Make-Over

A garage can be used to house your car, sporting equipment, tools, or, just stuff. Everything can be organized to gain space, store items safely and make them visible for easy retrieval. Beginning with a design draft, we used sturdy shelving, MonkeyBar Storage bike and chair racks as well as additional wooden shelving. Items are easily identified and quickly found.

Categories and Placement

Categories of books, specific authors and favorites are positioned in the most easily accessible locations of this well-loved library. Each shelf is labeled to reflect its contents and a spreadsheet was created to serve as a quick reference guide to show where everything is placed.

Storage Ideas

Visible Storage

These Hefty plastic storage bins from Target are my favorite of choice today. The lids latch securely closed and offer extra space under the lid. The three sizes work nicely together to maximize space within shelves.

Separate & Find Easily

Safely stack those hard to fit items in your cabinets with simple dividers made to order. Inexpensive wood can be used to create easy access to platters and trays while keeping the larger items in their own space.

Easily Grab Spices

Spices can fit nicely stacked into a drawer. If you have the space, this is great alternative to a lazy susan for housing and being able to quickly locate a certain spice for that great recipe you're in the middle of.

Color and Convenience

Placed within open shelving, these decorative fabric or woven bins with built in handles create easily accessible storage that is also pleasing to the eye. Add color to a room or keep it neutral.

Vertical Tiered Files

If your stack of to-do's are piled flat on your desk, chances are you'll spend hours looking for that one thing. Store your files and bills in a tiered, sorted holder to find what you're looking for quickly. Colors and phrases that make sense to you will also help you locate paper items easily.
