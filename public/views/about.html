“Jill” of All Trades…

Over the years, I have worn many hats supporting a variety of jobs, gaining skills and utilizing my talents along the way, which has ultimately secured my decision to become a professional organizer. To date, my journey has included many upper level positions such as Executive Administrator, Administrative Assistant, Office Manager, Department Manager, Events Manager, Project Manager, and Contract Manager. In addition, an array of other titles such as Receptionist, Customer Service, Travel Agent and Animal Behavior Consultant need to also be mentioned. I am the mother of 2 wonderful boys and have been happily married for 30 years. Participating in my boy’s childhood activities invited me to become a Cub Scout Den Leader, Team mom for soccer and baseball as well as a caregiver within my own family home daycare program. Today, I love the outdoors, photography, crafts, gardening and my furry family members. Can you see where I was headed all along?

Details, keeping things in order and offering simpler ways to achieve better results have always been what I have been most proud of. With many years invested in producing how-to manuals and simple forms, I've learned to carefully listen to what is needed in order to remedy a particular situation.
